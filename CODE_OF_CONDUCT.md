# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, technical and evidence-oriented. Critique decisions and artifacts, not people.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, deliberate disclosure of confidential information and bad-faith manipulation of evidence are not accepted. Maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions and restrict participation when conduct threatens the safety or integrity of the project.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Enforcement procedures and an independent escalation path will be documented as the maintainer group grows.
